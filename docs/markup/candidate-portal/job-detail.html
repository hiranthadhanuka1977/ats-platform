Senior Business Analyst

Product & Delivery Colombo, Sri Lanka Full-time 5+ Years

Job Overview

We are seeking a Senior Business Analyst to lead requirement gathering and stakeholder coordination for our enterprise platform initiatives. This role bridges business needs and technology delivery, ensuring solutions are well-scoped, documented, and aligned with organizational goals.

Role Summary

The candidate will work closely with business and technical teams to deliver enterprise solutions. You will own the end-to-end analysis lifecycle — from discovery workshops through to delivery support — and act as a trusted advisor to product owners and engineering leads.

Key Responsibilities

  • Gather and document functional and non-functional requirements from stakeholders
  • Conduct stakeholder discussions, workshops, and discovery sessions
  • Prepare detailed business requirements documents (BRDs) and user stories
  • Create process flows, wireframes, and data models to support solution design
  • Collaborate with QA teams to define acceptance criteria and test scenarios
  • Track requirement changes and manage scope through delivery cycles
  • Present findings and recommendations to senior leadership

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Business, or a related field
  • 5+ years of experience in a Business Analyst or similar role
  • Proven track record working with cross-functional teams in Agile environments
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Experience with requirement management tools (e.g., Jira, Confluence)

Preferred Qualifications

  • CBAP or PMI-PBA certification
  • Experience with enterprise SaaS products
  • Familiarity with data analytics and reporting tools
  • Background in HR tech or workforce management domains

Required Skills

Requirement Analysis Stakeholder Management User Story Writing Process Modelling Agile / Scrum Jira & Confluence Data Analysis Wireframing

What We Offer

  • Career growth opportunities with clear progression paths
  • Flexible work arrangements and remote-friendly policies
  • Health and wellness benefits
  • Learning and development budget
  • Collaborative, inclusive team culture
