## About this list

Tags surface quick signals on job cards or detail pages—such as **On-site**, **Hybrid**, or **Remote**.

- **Variant** — Maps to UI styling (`primary`, `accent`, `success`, `warning`). Align variants with your design system.
- **Sort order** — Controls order when multiple tags are shown.
- **Name** — Unique; keep labels concise.
