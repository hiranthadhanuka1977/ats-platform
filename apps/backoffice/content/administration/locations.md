## About this list

Locations describe where a role is based or where your team hires from.

- **Slug** — Unique per location; often derived from city and country (for example `colombo-lk`). Keep it stable once postings reference it.
- **Active** — Use to retire a location without deleting historical postings that still reference it.
- **Sort order** — How locations appear in filters and dropdowns.
