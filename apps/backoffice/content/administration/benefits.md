## About this list

Benefits appear in the “What we offer” section on job detail views. Recruiters pick from this list when editing a posting.

- **Description** — Short label shown to candidates (max 255 characters).
- **Sort order** — Lower numbers appear first in pickers and on the job page.
- Deleting a benefit removes it from the shared list; existing postings lose that benefit line until you add another.
