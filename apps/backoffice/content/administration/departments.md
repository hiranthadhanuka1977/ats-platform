## About this list

Departments group roles for internal reporting and help candidates browse open positions by area.

- **Slug** — Stable, URL-safe identifier. Prefer lowercase kebab-case (for example `engineering`). Changing slugs later can break bookmarks or integrations.
- **Active** — Inactive departments stay in the database but are hidden from pickers when creating new postings.
- **Sort order** — Controls how departments appear in dropdowns (lower numbers first).
