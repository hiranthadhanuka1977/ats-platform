## About this list

Each job posting belongs to one company. Candidates see the company name (and optional logo and website) on job details.

- **Logo** — URL to an image file (PNG, SVG, or JPG). Use HTTPS links hosted on your CDN or careers site.
- **Website** — Public company or careers page URL.
- **Active** — Inactive companies stay in the database but are hidden from job posting pickers.
