## About this list

Experience levels set expectations for candidates and help match roles to the right audience.

- **Slug** — Stable identifier (for example `mid-level`).
- **Minimum years** — A non-negative hint used for filtering or display; align labels with your internal leveling guidelines.
- **Sort order** — Typically ascending from junior to senior.
