Critical findings

C1

No functional privacy policy or terms

GDPR Art. 13–14 · PDPA S.7–8

SiteFooter.tsx in candidate-portal and my-applications uses href="#" for Privacy, Terms, and Accessibility. Register requires agreeing to policies but they are not linked in RegisterForm.tsx.

Risk: Processing without accessible privacy information before collection.

Remediation: Publish /privacy and /terms — TH-192; link from footer, registration, and apply flow.
C2

No cookie / consent mechanism

GDPR Art. 6(1)(a), Art. 7 · PDPA S.6–7

No consent banner or preference centre in any Next.js app. Non-essential cookies, analytics, or third-party requests should be blocked until consent.

Remediation: Implement consent UI; default-deny non-essential scripts; document essential vs optional cookies.
C3

Backoffice loads Google Fonts from CDN

GDPR Art. 44–49 · PDPA S.25

backoffice/src/app/globals.css imports fonts.googleapis.com. Each page load can send visitor IP to Google (US) without notice or consent. Portal and my-applications use next/font.

Remediation: Mirror candidate-portal layout — self-host via next/font/google.
C4

OpenAI processing without privacy notice or consent

GDPR Art. 13(1)(e), Art. 28, Art. 9 · PDPA S.8(1)(d), S.5

CV parse, screenshot extract, and relevance scoring routes send content to OpenAI. AiRelevanceBiasNotice is product transparency only — not Art. 13/22 legal disclosure. No privacy policy or opt-in before sending CV/screenshot content.

Remediation: Name OpenAI in privacy policy; lawful basis; explicit checkbox before AI-assisted import/scoring; DPA with OpenAI; data minimization.
C5

Candidate session tokens in browser storage

GDPR Art. 32 · PDPA S.18

auth-storage.ts — JWT in localStorage / sessionStorage. Any XSS can exfiltrate tokens.

Remediation: HttpOnly cookies from BFF or API — TH-193; shorten TTL; CSP hardening.
C6

Apply / registration without layered consent

GDPR Art. 6(1)(a), Art. 7, Art. 9 · PDPA S.6

Apply flow collects extensive PII (CV, cover letter, salary, work authorization) without just-in-time notice or separate consent for recruitment pool / AI / marketing.

Remediation: Before submit: purpose, categories, retention, recipients, privacy policy link, required checkbox(es).
